About Us

Welcome to Furniture Store, where quality meets comfort. We are committed to providing our customers with the finest furniture to create beautiful and functional spaces. Our team of dedicated professionals works tirelessly to bring you the best products and services in the industry.

Core Purpose

To provide a smart, easy, and joyful lifestyle.

Vision

To be the most trusted omni-channel retailer in the home-office improvements segment, trusted by our team, and customers in equal measure.

Mission

Our mission is to sustainably deliver the pleasure and benefits of furniture to as many people as possible. We believe in the power of beautiful spaces and are dedicated to helping you create the perfect environment for your home or office.

Value & Belief

  • CSR
  • Unequivocal excellence in all aspects of the company
  • Excellence in reputation
  • Continually improve the value of the products and services offered to the customers
  • Online and technology-based
  • Honesty and integrity
  • Encouraging individual initiative and productivity
  • Maintain an organizational environment that fosters individual motivation, initiative, and creativity
  • Opportunity for everyone based on merit and self-motive
  • Continues training for self-improvement
  • Demonstrate good citizenship by making contributions to the community

BHAG

  • To become the IKEA of India
  • To become the largest social opportunity provider in the home-office improvement segment

Vivid Description

  • We will design produce and deliver furniture and interior ideas
  • We will integrate different types of retail stores, customer experience centers, and self-motivated enthusiastic marketing individuals and customers through our own online network
  • We will have 150 tie-up stores across Kerala
  • We will have e-stores in each Gramma Panchayath of Kerala
  • We will have experience centers in all regions
  • We will have service store franchisees across Kerala
